• TOP10 GHG Emission

China, USA, European Union (28 countries), India, Russian, Indonesia, Brazil, Japan, Canada and Mexico are top 10 Greenhouse Gas Emission countries. These countries occupy around 70 percent of global GHG emissions. GHG emission changes of top 10 countries are presented in each small chart.
China's GHG emission, obviously experienced a most dramatic increase, becoming NO.1 GHG emission country since 2005. Even though European Union's emission ranks as the third, the amount of emission does not change a lot throughout years.
