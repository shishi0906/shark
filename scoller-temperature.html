Looking At Most Recent Years
Based on records, thirteen of the 14 hottest years are in the 21st century, especially recent years (since 2010), temperatures increase almost every year. As expected, the temperature in 2015 reached the peak point in the history of record.
Looking At The Start Point
1880- 1882 are the most oldest years in the dataset, they locate at the bottom of chart, which demonstrate that temperatures at that period are much lower than today.
Looking At The Lowest Years
1909- 1912 are basically the coldest years, there is an around 150°C increases between the highest and lowest point.
